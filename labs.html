OUR ADVANCED LABORATORY EQUIPMENT

01. Advanced Microscopy

At Lokkalyan Diagnostics, precision is key, and our advanced microscopes play a vital role in delivering accurate diagnostic results. Microscopy allows our expert pathologists to closely examine blood cells, tissues, and microorganisms, helping to detect a wide range of medical conditions, including infections, cancers, and blood disorders. Our cutting-edge microscopes offer high-resolution imaging, ensuring that even the smallest cellular abnormalities are identified with clarity. With the aid of these powerful tools, our diagnostic team can make informed decisions, providing patients with reliable results and guiding physicians in determining the best course of treatment.

02. Elitech Biochemistry Analyzer

At Lokkalyan Diagnostics, we use the state-of-the-art Elitech Fully Automatic Biochemistry Analyzer to ensure precise and efficient testing. This advanced equipment automates the analysis of blood and body fluids, enabling quick and accurate assessments of various biochemical parameters, including liver function, kidney function, lipid profiles, blood glucose levels, and more. The Elitech analyzer allows for: High Accuracy: Automated processes reduce human error, ensuring reliable results every time. Fast Turnaround: With the capability to process multiple tests simultaneously, we deliver reports promptly without compromising quality. Wide Range of Tests. .

03. Real-Time PCR System

At Lokkalyan Diagnostics, we utilize the Qiagen Real-Time PCR System—a world-class technology from Germany—offering unmatched precision in molecular diagnostics. This cutting-edge system allows us to detect and quantify genetic material (DNA or RNA) in real-time, providing highly sensitive and specific results, especially in the diagnosis of infections, genetic disorders, and cancer. Key features of our Real-Time PCR System: High Sensitivity: Capable of detecting even the smallest amounts of genetic material, making it ideal for early diagnosis of viral and bacterial infections, such as COVID-19, HIV, hepatitis, and more. Rapid Results: The real-time monitoring of amplification processes ensures faster turnaround times, delivering timely results for critical conditions. .

04. Multipara Analyzer

Lokkalyan Diagnostics, the Multipara Analyzer is a crucial tool for performing comprehensive and rapid testing in critical situations. This advanced device is designed to simultaneously analyze multiple parameters from a single sample, providing fast and accurate results for patients requiring urgent medical attention. Key features of our Multipara Analyzer: Multi-Parameter Testing: This system assesses several key biomarkers such as blood gases, electrolytes, glucose, lactate, and more in one test, making it indispensable for critically ill patients. Rapid Turnaround: With the ability to deliver results in just minutes, the Multipara Analyzer ensures that healthcare providers can make quick, informed decisions during emergency situations. Comprehensive Diagnostics: It is widely used for managing patients in ICU, emergency rooms, and operating theaters, where continuous monitoring of parameters like pH, oxygen levels, and electrolytes is essential. .
