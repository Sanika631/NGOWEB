About Sushruta

Project Sushruta was launched in August 2023 with the mission of delivering essential healthcare services, especially in underserved regions. The objective is to raise awareness and provide early detection of genetic disorders for improved health outcomes.

Impact and Reach

Since its inception, Project Sushruta has screened nearly 23,000 individuals out of 25,000. Early detection and preventive healthcare are the core achievements of this initiative.

Community Engagement

Sushruta organizes awareness sessions and provides affordable or free medical services, empowering communities under the mission: "Healthy Youth - Healthy India".

Focus Area

Rural & Underprivileged Regions

High-risk areas lacking basic healthcare access.

Adolescent Health

Screening young individuals for Thalassemia and Sickle Cell.

Preventive Health

Health camps & awareness drives for early intervention.

Key Services Provided

Awareness Programs

Education about genetic disorders.

Screening Camps

Regular health camps for Thalassemia & Sickle Cell detection.

Counseling Services

Professional guidance for diagnosed individuals.

Diagnostic Services

Affordable lab tests for rural families.

Home Sample Collection

Support for patients with limited mobility.

Facilities & Infrastructure

Lokkalyan Diagnostics supports the project with 25+ years of medical and social service experience, offering accurate diagnostic reports.
